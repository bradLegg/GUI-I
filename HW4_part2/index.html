• Dynamic Multiplication Table

Rules:

Inputs must be integers between -50 and 50.

Min-Cols (Minimum-Columns) implies a range of multiplication table columns starting at the entered value.

Max-Cols (Maximum-Columns) implies a range of multiplication table columns ending at the entered value.

Min-Rows (Minimum-Rows) implies a range of multiplication table rows starting at the entered value.

Max-Rows (Maximum-Rows) implies a range of multiplication table rows ending at the entered value.

Note: Min-Cols is less than or equal to Max-Cols & Min-Rows is less than or equal to Max-Rows.
